Mentamizh - Tamil Word Processor

Our flagship product, Mentamizh, is a state-of-the-art Tamil word processor that meets international standards. In 2013, it received the prestigious "Tamil Nadu Chief Minister Award for Tamil Computing." The Government of Tamil Nadu has adopted this software for use in its offices across the state.

MS Office Add-in Tools

All the language tools available in our Tamil word processor are also provided as add- in tools for MS Office software, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Publisher.

Spell Checker

Our team has developed a rule-based spell checker for modern written Tamil. Creating this tool required a robust morphological parser to handle Tamil's agglutinative and inflectional nature. A typical Tamil word-form can contain up to twelve suffixes attached to the root, adhering to strict morphotactic and morphophonological rules. Key components of our development process include: Morphological Parser: Essential for processing Tamil's complex word formations. Comprehensive Lexicon: Incorporating all necessary details, including noun declensions and verb conjugations. Built on this foundation of Tamil computational morphology, our spell checker effectively identifies errors in Tamil text and provides precise correction suggestions. It also identifies and corrects internal sandhi errors and sandhi between word-forms. This tool maximizes both recall and precision in error detection and correction, processing over one hundred words per second. Additionally, unnecessary combinations of lexicons in the text are separated according to Tamil morphology to achieve a simpler style in Tamil texts.

Tool For AutoSuggestion

For the first time in the history of Tamil computational linguistics and language technology, we have introduced a unique application: Auto-Suggestion. This feature encompasses nearly all the inflected forms of every lexicon found in our dictionary.

Easy Transliteration from Tamil Text to Roman Script

Our software simplifies the process of transliterating Tamil text into Roman script. It supports both Roman and phonetic transcription, making the conversion seamless and efficient.

Corpus Creation and Analysis

Our advanced language tools facilitate the creation and analysis of Tamil corpus. Effortlessly perform cleaning, normalization, tokenization, type selection, and lexicon extraction for Tamil. Additionally, we offer a comprehensive suite of language analysis tools, including morphological parsing, parts of speech (POS) tagging, N-gram analysis, concordance, and colligation. From phonological analysis to discourse analysis, our software is designed to handle all aspects of written Tamil text analysis with precision and ease.

E-Dictionary

Our E-Dictionary projects include a Tamil suspicious words dictionary, synonym and antonym dictionaries, and a dictionary for non-native to native words.
